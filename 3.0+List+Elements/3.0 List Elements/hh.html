My top 3 movies of all time

Sanam Teri Kasam

It's a slow burn kind of movie, at the start it feels like just another ordinary movie, however slowly you feel so drawn towards it that you are in awe and tears by the end. It's one of those kinds of movies which will just make you deeply sad but also will give a lesson about the meaning of true love and how gaining and losing it feels like. Definitely one of the all time greats according to me. I am so glad people are recognizing it's worth now.
